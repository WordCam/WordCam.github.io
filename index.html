Word Cam

Quick one word dictionary lookups from camera pictures

Why one word lookups?

Word Cam enables you to quickly lookup word definitions when reading a paperback book or other non-digital reading material without having to type in the word. Word Cam seamlessly combines enhanced black-and-white images with text extraction (image OCR) and a dictionary lookup.
